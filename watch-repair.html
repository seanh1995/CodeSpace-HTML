Watch Repair Service

Our professional watch repair service ensures your timepiece is restored to its original working condition. Whether it’s a simple battery replacement or a complete overhaul, we take care of every detail to keep your watch running like new.

Our Services Include:

  • Battery Replacement: Replace your watch battery with high-quality replacements for accurate timekeeping.
  • Strap Repair & Replacement: We offer leather, metal, and rubber strap repairs and replacements.
  • Water Resistance Testing: Ensure your watch is waterproof with a full testing and sealing process.
  • Movement Repair: Complete mechanical repair or servicing for watches with automatic, manual, or quartz movements.
  • Crystal Replacement: Clear and scratch-free crystal replacements to restore your watch's appearance.
  • Full Watch Servicing: A full service that includes cleaning, oiling, and adjusting the internal mechanisms.
