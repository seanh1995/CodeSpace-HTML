Who We Are

Nestled in the historic heart of Edinburgh, MK Times is a sanctuary for those who appreciate the artistry and precision of fine timepieces. As a family-owned business, we have deep roots in Scotland’s capital, and our passion for watches transcends generations. Our collection is a carefully curated blend of craftsmanship, innovation, and style, designed to cater to both seasoned collectors and those who are new to the world of horology.

At MK Times, we pride ourselves on offering a wide array of timepieces—from classic, heirloom-worthy watches to cutting-edge, luxury designs that make a statement. We believe that every watch has a story to tell, and we are here to help you find the one that resonates with your own journey. Whether you're looking for a sophisticated dress watch, a robust sports model, or a sleek everyday companion, our selection is thoughtfully chosen to meet every taste and need.

Our commitment to excellence goes beyond the watches we offer. We provide personalized service with a focus on educating and guiding our customers through the diverse world of watches. Each member of our team is an expert in the field, with an eye for detail and a passion for helping you discover a timepiece that fits your lifestyle. Our goal is not just to sell watches but to ensure that each customer leaves feeling confident in their choice and connected to the craftsmanship behind it.

MK Times is more than a boutique—it's a celebration of time itself. As a testament to Scotland’s rich heritage of precision and artistry, we carry only the finest brands and independent watchmakers, each of whom shares our dedication to quality and innovation. From Swiss craftsmanship to British ingenuity, the watches we offer reflect the best of both tradition and modernity.

Step into MK Times, and you'll find more than a timepiece; you'll discover a lasting investment in quality, style, and legacy. Join us in celebrating the remarkable stories behind the watches we wear, and let us help you find the perfect piece that will stand the test of time.
